Admission Procedure:

Admission to the B.Tech. Biotechnology Program at G.B. Pant University of Agriculture and technology takes place through the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Mains.

Fee Structure:

Fee to be paid once at admission:

Placement Counseling Fees : Rs. 1000
Caution Money (Refundable) : Rs. 2500
Admission Fees : Rs. 250
Alumni Fees (4A) : Rs. 500
Subtotal : Rs. 4250

Annual Fee:

Magazine Fee : Rs. 100
Students Aid Fund : Rs. 100
Students Society Fee : Rs. 200
Student Hostel Maintenance Fund : Rs. 1500
E-mail/Internet Fee (CCF) : Rs. 1000
Computer Charge and Stationary Fee : Rs. 500
Med Claim : Rs. 374
Annual Alumni Meet (PTAA) : Rs. 200
Subtotal : Rs. 3974

Semester Fee:

Tuition Fee: Rs. 25000
Library Fee: Rs. 400
Medical Fee: Rs.250
Games Fee: Rs. 250
Laboratory Fee: Rs. 250
Examination Fee: Rs. 1000
Room Rent: Rs. 300
Tour Fee: Rs. 300
Electrical Charges
(i) Single Seated Rooms : Rs. 1500
(ii) Double Seated Rooms : Rs. 1000
(iii) Triple Seated Rooms : Rs. 750
Water Charges : Rs. 60

In addition to the fee above, students have to pay food advance of Rs. 20,000/- each semester.
