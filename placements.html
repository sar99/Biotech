Procedure for Placement

  • 1

    Organisations are welcome to make a pre-placement talk/visit, which provides an opportunity for the organiza on to showcase their corporate requirement and job profiles.

  • 2

    Prospective employers intimate the Director about their human resource requirements in various disciplines running in the University.

  • 3

    The Directorate there upon invites the bio-data of interested students and pass on to the prospective employers.

  • 4

    A mutually agreed date for holding interviews either at the university campus or employers’ head-quarters is fixed and the candidates are directed accordingly.

  • 5

    For interviews held at Pantnagar, the University provides free boarding and lodging facilities to the members of the Selection Committee in the University.

  • 6

    Besides recruitment related activities, companies are welcome to visit for academia-industry interface and meet faculty members for exploring new areas of collaboration in research and consultancy projects.
